About Me

Driven by a profound commitment to health equity and patient-centered care, I bring a unique blend of clinical experience, rigorous scientific research, and compassionate community advocacy. My journey has focused on addressing health disparities, from directly assisting vulnerable patients and leading community mental health initiatives to investigating neurobiological mechanisms of addiction and advancing safer medical practices through data analysis.

I am passionate about leveraging interdisciplinary approaches to solve complex healthcare challenges and foster inclusive, supportive environments where every individual feels seen, heard, and cared for. My experiences have reinforced my belief in the transformative power of empathy, data-driven solutions, and collective action to drive meaningful change in healthcare.
